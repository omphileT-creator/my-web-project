Bubbly Cup Of


Almond Black Milk Tea 🧋

The drink that started it all is the classic black milk tea. Traditionally this is made with black tea and shaken with a non-dairy creamer; creating bubbles on top on the tea. There are multiple versions of this drink now with so many different toppings/sinkers and flavors. Some of our customers favorite flavors are taro, almond, rose, caramel, and coconut. We have a wide range of flavors of milk teas and it’s honestly up to your preference. We sweeten it where we feel has a nice balance of sweetness and tea. If you don’t like your drinks too sweet or you’re just trying to cut back, you can always ask us to cut the sugar in quarters, half, etc. We also have sweet n low, stevia, and splenda available.
